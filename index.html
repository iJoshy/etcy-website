The Principal

Joshua Balogun

Joshua is a technology leader with over 15 years of experience architecting enterprise-scale systems across fintech, telecommunications, and the public sector. He founded Etcy Systems to bring senior engineering rigour and strategic clarity to organisations navigating consequential digital decisions.

His career spans landmark digital deployments — from Nigeria's first mobile self-care applications for Etisalat, to complex payment infrastructure at eTranzact, to enterprise financial platforms at SystemSpecs. He holds a Master of Science in Computer Science from Georgia Institute of Technology and a degree in Computer Engineering from Covenant University.

His mission is direct: "World Impact with Code."
